Description

Conny is a famous singer in Myanmar. She sang many songs with these categories like Heavy Metal, Popand many others. She is one of the most famous female singer in Myanmar. From the early of 1980 to the end of 1990 can be called her years. At first, she was the background singer of New Wave Band . At the middle of 1980, she started becoomibg a famous singer. At the year 1886, she realesed her very first album and the writer was Thu Kha Main Hlaing. After then, she continue singing and realsed many albums. All of her albums are a success. She collaborate with many famous singer of that time like Tue Tue, Hay Mar Nay Win,Chit Kaung and created many famous songs of those time. After 1990, she disappeared from the music field. But she released a song named Sayy(ဆေး) at 2019. It was also become a hit. Many people loved to sing her music. Despite of her age, her voice remains calm and powerful as before. She announced that she will make One Lady Show soon.
